Sustainabilty at your fingertips

We want to help people to have greater environmental awareness by avoiding climate-damaging goods and buying more sustainable products.
Team GreenPenguin

In a team of 6 people we designed an interface for a mobile app that allows you to scan products, get information about their sustainability and compare them to other products. In this project we went through the whole design process from user research to prototyping.

We started with a few sessions of the so called "crazy 8's" method, where every team member has 8 minutes to sketch 8 screen spontaniously. After that we discussed the sketches and decided which ones we want to develop further.

With the help of user tests and feedback, we could improve and optimize our design step by step. Here you can see the development of our main screen from low high-fidelity to final design.
