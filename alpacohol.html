Alpacohol - Tracker
Know your limits!

Alpacohol-Tracker is an Android application that allows you to track your alcohol consumption.

After you have entered your weight and gender, you can choose the drink you have consumed ...

... or create your own drink with an image from your gallery or camera.

Alpacohol calculates your blood alcohol content with the Widmark formular and shows you how much time you have to wait until you are sober again.
