Habit-Tracker

"You'll never change your life until you change something you do daily."
John C. Maxwell

Habit-Tracker is a software that allows you to improve your weelbeeing by having a better overview over your habits and feelings.

You can enter your habit and the days when you want to do it.

To understand your feelings better, you can write small notes about your day in the journal.
